Our Story

From One Warehouse To Seven Brands

LINGARAJA started as a single hardware stockist supplying contractors around Lucknow. As demand grew for tools, pumps, cement, TMT, bathware and uPVC systems that a single dealer could vouch for, we built two things: our own house brands — BULL, ECO and WORTEX — for the categories where we could control quality end-to-end, and authorised dealerships with ACC, ASTRAL, GOVINDA and KAKA for the categories where a manufacturer relationship matters more.

Every SKU across all seven brands is catalogued against its HS classification and backed by the same sales desk — so whether you're buying an angle grinder or a truckload of TMT bar, the support experience doesn't change.
